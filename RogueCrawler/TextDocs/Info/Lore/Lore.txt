On The Races

Humans, Elves, and Dwarves are all, at their core, humans.
Elves and Dwarves are descendencts of the humans that served Angels and Demons respectively during the ancient wars between the two.
They have been permanently altered in body and mind by the magics wielded at the time.

Angels and Demons are to Fae what Elves and Dwarves are to humans.
Angels and Demons are the descendants of Greater Fae that commanded and fought in the ancient wars. At the time, they were just influential families or individuals.
Largely nobility and merchants. There were also great wizards, engineers, and researchers that were recruited or conscripted.
The magics they discovered, invented, and wielded permanently altered them in body and mind to approximate the gods themselves.
Unfortuntaely, this made them susceptable to direct godly influence, and these two factions were ultimately forced to serve the very gods they sought to harness.
Those that resisted were enslaved, and eventually all of them were coerced into leaving the mortal plane into the respective of their masters.
Today, Angels and Demons can still be found in the mortal plane. Though exceptionally powerful and dangerous, they are largely passive entities that will only interfere with mortals that intefere with whatever task their master has given them.
In general, Angels are more likely to reason with mortals who have accidentally stumbled upon them, or cease chasing them once they are driven from the area. Demons are more likely to eliminate them entirely, often quickly and without warning. Keep in mind, that this is just a general pattern of behaviour. There is no guarentee an Angel will listen to your pleas, or that a Demon wont.
