Attributes: 
	STR -> Physical strength. Carry load, heavy armor, hand-to-hand damage, and dmg bonus for some weapon types.
	DEX -> Dexterity. Represents your ability to move yourself, fine motor control, and how familliar you are with your body and its capabilities.
	CON -> Represents endurance. How much damage you can take through conditioning. Can also be interpreted as your resistance to afflictions from various sources.
	INT -> Represents intelligence; book smarts. High INT implies your character is knowledgable in magic and how it functions on a technical level.  
	WIS -> Represents experience. Wisdom influences many aspects of a character. Life experience touches everything your character does, from knowing when to take a blow vs block it, or how much power to put into a spell for optimal effects.
	CHA -> Represents charisma. Charisma allows you to convince people, gods, and in some cases, even the fabric of reality itself into granting you favorable terms and outcomes.

HP = STR * 2.5 + (CON+1) * 5
FP = CON * 2.5 + (DEX+1) * 5
MP = ((INT+CHA) * 2) + (WIS+1) * 5

Combat Speed = 1 + (DEX * 2 + WIS + (CHA /2)) / 10
	Your actions per round is equal to the floor of your speed value.
	Anything above the floor becomes a % chance to get an additonal action.
	In battle, combat order is determined by DESCENDING speed order. 
	A creature with 2.5 speed goes before one with 2.4 speed, and so on.

Exp Need Per level: 100 + ((Level - 1) * 200)
	1 -> 100
	2 -> 300
	3 -> 500
	4 -> 700
