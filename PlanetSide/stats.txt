Kills - Infantry Kills
Deaths - True death count, minus TKs
TeamKills - Infantry TKs
Headshots - Number of headshot kills from all sources

VehicleKills - Number of GROUND vehicles killed
VehicleDeaths - Number of Ground vehicles lost, minus TKs
VehicleTeamKills - Number of ground vehicles lost to Tks

AirKills - Number of Air vehicles killed
AirDeaths - Number of Air vehicles lost, minus TKs
AirTeamKills - Number of Air vehicles lost to TKs

ReviveScore - Revives divided by deaths. Basically Rez efficiency. 

Repair (Any category) - Total cumulative score gained
Resupplies - Total amount of resupply ticks
